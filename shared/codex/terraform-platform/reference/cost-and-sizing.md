# Cost and Sizing

## Cost Hygiene

- Terraform can encode waste just as easily as value
- Review defaults for oversized instances, disks, databases, and retention settings
- Check whether autoscaling, schedules, or tiering belong in the design

## Review Areas

- Idle always-on resources
- Oversized compute
- Excessively replicated storage or logging
- Missing lifecycle rules or retention controls
- Redundant network or edge resources

## Rule

Reduce waste without hiding reliability risk or operational intent.
